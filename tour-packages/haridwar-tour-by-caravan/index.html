About Haridwar
Tour by Caravan

Experience the spiritual heart of India with our premium Haridwar tour by caravan. Haridwar, the "Gateway to God," is one of the seven holiest places for Hindus. While thousands visit daily, our caravan service offers you a unique, private, and comfortable way to witness the city's divine energy.

Forget the hassle of crowded buses or expensive hotel bookings. Our luxury caravan rental from Delhi to Haridwar provides a "Hotel on Wheels" experience. You can relax in reclining seats, enjoy onboard snacks, and travel at your own pace, ensuring you arrive refreshed and ready for the Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri.

Whether you're planning a family pilgrimage, a solo spiritual quest, or a group trip, our Haridwar tour package covers all major landmarks including Mansa Devi Temple, Chandi Devi Temple, and the bustling local markets. With an expert chauffeur and 24/7 support, your safety and comfort are our top priorities.
