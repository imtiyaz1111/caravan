About Rishikesh
Tour by Caravan

Experience the spiritual and adventurous heart of the Himalayas with our premium Rishikesh tour by caravan. Known as the "Yoga Capital of the World," Rishikesh offers a unique blend of peace, spirituality, and thrill. Our caravan service makes this iconic journey from Delhi a seamless and luxury experience.

Why compromise on comfort during the 5-hour drive? Our luxury caravan rental from Delhi to Rishikesh serves as your private mobile retreat. Equipped with reclining leather seats, a climate-controlled cabin, and an onboard washroom, you can prepare for your rafting session or relax after a yoga retreat without any travel fatigue.

From the legendary Laxman Jhula to the soul-stirring Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat, our flexible itinerary puts you in the driver's seat. Whether you want to explore the Beatles Ashram or camp by the riverside, your luxury caravan provides the freedom to move at your own pace with the security of a private suite.
