About Char Dham
Yatra by Caravan

The Char Dham Yatra — visiting Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath — is considered the most sacred pilgrimage journey in Hinduism. Millions undertake this spiritual voyage every year to seek blessings and moksha. Our premium caravan service makes this divine experience more comfortable, personal, and stress-free than ever before.

Forget crowded buses and tiring hotel stays. With our Char Dham Yatra caravan from Delhi, your vehicle is your home on wheels. Equipped with reclining seats, onboard kitchen, private washroom, and entertainment system, you travel at your own pace while maintaining the sanctity and purpose of your pilgrimage.

Our chauffeurs are experienced hill drivers who know the Uttarakhand mountain routes well, ensuring your safety on every hairpin bend. We cover all four dhams in a single carefully planned itinerary, stopping at the right places for rest, darshan, and sightseeing.

The Four Sacred Dhams

Tour Highlights

Visit all four holy shrines of the Char Dham circuit in complete luxury and comfort.

1st Dham

Yamunotri

The source of the Yamuna River. Witness the sacred Divya Shila rock and Surya Kund hot spring. Altitude: 3,293 m.

2nd Dham

Gangotri

Origin of the sacred Ganges River. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Ganga amid breathtaking Himalayan scenery.

3rd Dham

Kedarnath

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. Set against the majestic Kedarnath peak at 3,583 m above sea level.

4th Dham

Badrinath

Abode of Lord Vishnu, nestled between Nar and Narayan mountain ranges. One of the most visited pilgrimage sites in India.
