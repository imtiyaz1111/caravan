About Shimla
Tour by Caravan

Experience the timeless elegance of the "Queen of Hills" with our premium Shimla tour by caravan. Once the summer capital of British India, Shimla still retains its colonial charm through its majestic architecture and pedestrian-friendly Mall Road. Our caravan service turns this classic mountain trip into a modern luxury journey.

Forget the fatigue of navigating mountain curves in a cramped car. Our luxury caravan rental from Delhi to Shimla offers a "Lounge on Wheels." With high-altitude stability, ergonomic reclining seats, and a temperature-controlled cabin, you can enjoy the shifting landscapes of the Shivalik ranges in absolute peace.

From the bustling Mall Road to the adventurous slopes of Kufri, our flexible itinerary ensures you don't miss a thing. Wake up to cedar forests, enjoy a hot cup of coffee from your onboard kitchenette, and explore historic landmarks like the Viceregal Lodge at your own pace. With our expert chauffeurs, your safety on the hills is our priority.
